Privacy at GoalGate

Last updated July 11, 2026

What we collect

GoalGate processes the match questions you submit, technical request metadata required to secure the service, and public blockchain transaction references used to verify x402 payments. We do not require an account for pay-per-query API access.

Wallet and payment data

Wallet addresses and Injective transactions are public blockchain data. GoalGate stores only the transaction reference and payment status needed to deliver and audit a paid insight. Never submit private keys or seed phrases.

Service operations

Short-lived logs may contain IP address, request ID, route, response status, and timing information. They are used for security, abuse prevention, and reliability.

Your choices

You may use free fixture data without a wallet. Paid endpoints require a payment signature, but do not require a GoalGate profile.
